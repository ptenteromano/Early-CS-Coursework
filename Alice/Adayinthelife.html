Life in Manhattan

There Is definitely no place like New York. Here in the big apple, there are plenty of things to do and places to see. With a massively diverse population and one of the most densely populated areas in the world, NYC is always busy.



Some things to do:
Check out the Empire State Building!

Go see a hockey game at Madison Square Garden!

Visit one of the hundreds of public parks!

One World Trade Center Observatory!

Kayak on the Hudson River!

Ride a bicycle around town!
